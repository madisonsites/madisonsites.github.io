Early in 2015…

I (Madison) stepped away from a path to a VP of Marketing role and over into web development. I had already learned a lot from my diverse and global experiences in customer service and marketing, but I came to learn what I really wanted in a career:

  • 1 A Company that gives Every Dollar Employee a Job of prioritizing people: customers and each other
  • 2 A field where I could Embrace My True Expenses Love of learning
  • 3 A team that Rolls With The Punches - they value honesty, mistakes, and teaching
  • 4 A sense of Aging My Money Self well with work-life balance and flexibility

Living this way, and seeing how absolutely invaluable the Four Rules were, I've truly understood what it means to love what you do.
